Game Rules

  1. Your health is the amount of cards you have.
  2. Play your cards to steal your opponent's cards.
  3. You win when your opponent doesn't have any more cards.
  4. There are 36 cards in total.
  5. Each card has a buff that impacts your opponent's cards and their ability to use specials.
  6. Each buff comes from a previously defeated monster.
  7. Each time you play a card, one random card of your opponent reveals its sign.
