Choose Your Challenge Cards

Pick 2 cards from your hand to place on the playing field. The monster will do the same.

Suit power: Square beats Circle and Triangle. Circle beats Triangle. Triangle only beats Triangle.

Number: Within the same suit, the card with the higher number wins.

Strategy: Place cards high enough that the monster can take them — but low enough that you can take them back.

+ cards: Triangles marked with a "+" take an extra higher-suit card from the field after a successful attack.
